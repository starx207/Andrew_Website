Seriously, though

My name's Andrew Starr and I'm a software developer. I recieved my Bachelor of Science from Bowling Green State University in December of 2015. Six months later, I started my first software dev job at Appraisal Research Corporation.

What I love about programming is the ability to create something from nothing. You think about other types of engineers - electrical, mechanical, civil - and they all have something in common: they take one type of material and use it to make something else. Software Engineers, however, get to create whatever we want from nothing! I find it exciting that, in the virtual space, the only limitations are what we impose on ourselves.
